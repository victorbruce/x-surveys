X-Survey is a web application that help people get paid for taking surveys both online and offline. 

HOW DOES IT WORK?
1. You sign up online or subscribe through your mobile phone offline.
2. We push survey questions to you both online and through offline through Africa's talk API.
3. You will take the survey and submit your answers.
4. We pay you through mobile money using flutter wave API.
5. We then let your opinions make your rich and then Enjoy life! :)

demo-link: http://x-surveys.host.gl/
